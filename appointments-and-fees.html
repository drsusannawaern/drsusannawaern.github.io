Appointments and Fees

Appointments

Appointments are offered at two East London locations:

Liverpool Street
The Gestalt Centre
96-100 Clifton Street
London EC2A 4TP

Canary Wharf
Barkantine Practice
121 Westferry Road
London, E14 8JH

Appointments at home and in schools can also be offered, but charges for travel time may be added.

Appointment times are available Tuesday to Saturday, day time as well as evening time. Appointments via Skype or telephone consultations can also be arranged.

Fees

Fees vary depending on the service provided and whether formal reports are requested. Discounts are available for people who commit to an agreed block of sessions and for Skype appointments. Please contact Susanna for more information about fees.

Sessions cancelled less than 24 hours in advance will be charged at 100%. Sessions cancelled between 24 and 48 hours in advance will be charged at 50%. Sessions cancelled with more than 48 hours notice will not be charged for.

If you have private health insurance, please contact your provider for your authorisation number/pre- authorisation number. Susanna is registered with the following health insurance providers:

  • Bupa
  • AVIVA
  • Cigna
