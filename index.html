Psychological and neuropsychological assessments and interventions for children, adolescents and families.

Services

Psychological assessments and interventions can help with emotional and behavioural problems that are common in childhood and adolescence. Neuropsychological and cognitive assessments can establish a person's strength and weakness in terms of academic issues and help the child to progress academically through individually tailored support from school, family and therapists.
