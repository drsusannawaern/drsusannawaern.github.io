Consultation and Teaching

Consultation and teaching covering a range of topics is offered to NHS services, schools, universities and private organisations.

Consultation

Consultation regarding neuropsychological issues is offered to NHS services and schools where there is no access to a paediatric neuropsychologist, or where specialist knowledge about a certain condition is required.

The level of consultation can vary depending on the need of the service but can involve anything from a telephone conversation discussing test results to helping to plan an assessment.

Teaching

Teaching covering a range of topics can be offered, including paediatric psychology, paediatric neuropsychology, child development, emotional and behavioural problems in childhood, therapies for children and stress management.

Susanna has several years experience of providing consultation and teaching to a range of different professionals and institutions, including lecturing on topics such as Paediatric Psychology and Child Cognitive Development at University College London.
