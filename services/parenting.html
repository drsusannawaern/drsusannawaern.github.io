Parenting

Being a parent can be very stressful and especially if your child has a physical illness or special needs. Parenting sessions focus on understanding the meaning of your child's behaviour and finding strategies that can improve difficult behaviour. It focuses on building a relationship between a child and a parent that focuses on positive interactions.

Depending on the age of your child, your child may have their own therapy in conjunction with parenting sessions and some sessions may be done jointly.
